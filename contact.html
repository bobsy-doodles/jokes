Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit my own joke?

You can submit your own joke using the "Submit Your Joke" form on the home page. Fill in the setup, punchline, and category, and we'll add it to our collection!

How often are new jokes added?

We add new jokes regularly from user submissions and our own collection. Check back often for fresh content!

Can I use the jokes from this website?

Yes! Feel free to use and share the jokes. Just make sure to give credit to Just Jokes where possible.

How do I report offensive content?

If you find any offensive content, please use the contact form above to report it. Select "Bug Report" as the inquiry type and let us know.

Can I collaborate with Bobsy Doodles?

Absolutely! We'd love to collaborate. Use the contact form and select "Collaboration" as the inquiry type to discuss opportunities.
